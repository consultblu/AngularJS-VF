Fabric was only the start of our incredible journey 40 years ago.
The rest of our story begins with you…

In an industry known for it's fast-paced motion and aesthetic for highly designed spaces, Valley Forge is recognized as the sustainability forerunner in the production of high quality, decorative Fire Retardant fabrics worldwide. For 40 years, Valley Forge has specialized in the hospitality and lodging industries and is continually focused on exceptional design and product innovation. Over the years the company has grown to bring new product categories to the industry, including sustainable textiles, finished bedding, decorative sheers, coated blackouts and printed fabrics, and fabricated decorative roller shades.
